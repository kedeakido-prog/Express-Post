FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping and logistics services.

Tracking

You can track your shipment by entering your tracking number on our Track page. You will receive real-time updates on your package location, estimated delivery time, and complete shipment history.
Yes! Our advanced GPS tracking system provides real-time location updates for all shipments. You can also opt-in for SMS and email notifications at key delivery milestones.
If your tracking number shows no results, it may take up to 24 hours for the information to appear in our system after pickup. If the issue persists, please contact our support team.

Delivery

Delivery times vary based on the shipping method and destination. Express air freight typically takes 1-3 days, standard air freight 3-7 days, sea freight 15-45 days, and ground shipping 2-10 days depending on distance.
We ship to over 150 countries worldwide across all six continents. Our network includes major cities and remote locations. Contact us for specific destination availability.
Yes, we offer same-day delivery services in select metropolitan areas. This premium service is available for packages booked before 10 AM local time.

Security

We implement multi-layer security protocols including tamper-evident packaging, GPS tracking, secure warehouses with 24/7 surveillance, and comprehensive insurance coverage for all shipments.
Yes, all shipments include basic insurance coverage. Additional coverage options are available for high-value items. We recommend declared value insurance for valuable goods.
Fragile items receive special handling with custom packaging, "Fragile" labels, and dedicated storage areas. Our trained staff follows strict protocols to ensure safe delivery.

Pricing

Shipping costs are calculated based on package weight, dimensions, origin, destination, and selected service level. You can get an instant quote using our online calculator or by contacting sales.
We believe in transparent pricing. All costs including fuel surcharges, handling fees, and applicable taxes are disclosed upfront before you confirm your shipment.
Yes, we offer volume discounts for businesses with regular shipping needs. Contact our business team to discuss a customized pricing plan based on your shipping volume.
